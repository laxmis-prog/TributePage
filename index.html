Albert Einstein
German theoretical physicst, one of the most influentical scientist
14.03.1879-18.04.1955
Facts of Albert
  • Albert was born in Ulm in the Kingdom of Württemberg in the German Empire, on 14 March 1879.
  • Albert attended St. Peter Catholic elementary school in Munich from the age of five.
  • Einstein excelled at physics and mathematics from an early age.
  • He began teaching himself algebra, calculus and Euclidean geometry when he was twelve.
  • His love of algebra and geometry was so great that at twelve, he was already confident that nature could be understood as a "mathematical structure".
  • At seventeen, he enrolled in the four-year mathematics and physics teaching diploma program at the federal polytechnic school.
  • He met Serbian, Mileva Marić at federal polytechnic school, eventually the two students became not only friends but also lovers.
  • Einstein and Marić married in January 1903.
  • In 1912, Einstein entered into a relationship with Elsa Löwenthal, who was both his first cousin on his mother's side and his second cousin on his father's.
  • He was a talented violinist
  • Einstein had three nationalities. He was German, Swiss and American.
